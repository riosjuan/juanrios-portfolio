Llorona records is a label and a platform for creative projects around music based in Bogota - Colombia. Since 2007 Llorona Records, documents, promotes and represents unique and authentic artists.

The main purpose of thnsebsite is to promote the label artist, their music and the cultural projects of the foundation.

Part of the process was to make the site responsive. In Colombia, people that never had access to a regular computer have now a smartphones so it was quite important to make the site mobile friendly.
